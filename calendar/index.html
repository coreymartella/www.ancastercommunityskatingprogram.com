Skate Care tips:

  1. After skating, use a rag to dry the blades of the skates; this will help prevent rust from forming on the blades.
  2. Allow the blades to dry overnight before putting skate guards back on; this will help prevent rust from forming on the blades.
  3. Get the skates sharpened on a regular basis.
