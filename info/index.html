Below you will find descriptions of the different levels of skating we offer and what is required to achieve each level for both figure skating and power skating.

Figure Skating Badges

Pre-Beginner

  • sitting down and getting up
  • balance and posture
  • falling down
  • two foot jumping on the spot
  • turning on the spot (both directions)
  • forward skating across width of ice (alternate feet)

Beginner

  • left push-off (forward glide for three counts)
  • right push-off (forward glide for three counts)
  • step sequence (one count per step, minimum of six steps)
  • half snowplow stop (left foot in)
  • half snowplow stop (right foot in)
  • upright two foot glide into half sitting glide
  • backward two foot skating (L/R L/R or R/L R/L)

Elementary

  • two-foot spin (min of two rotations)
  • left push-off, right glide for four count, left stop
  • right push-off, left glide for four count, right stop
  • forward two-foot glide on a curve
  • forward one-foot glide on a curve
  • backward two foot skating (eight consecutive push glide sequences)
  • two-foot turn (forward to backward)
  • two-foot turn (backward to forward)
  • forward stroking across width of ice (two strokes per glide) with half snowplow stop
  • slalom (forward in any manner)

Basic

  • Forward bunny hop (left or right foot)
  • Forward circle thrusts (clockwise)
  • Forward crosscuts- clockwise (minimum six consecutive)
  • Two foot jump (forward to backward)
  • LFO - LBI three turn
  • RFO - RBI three turn
  • Forward stroking across width of ice (four counts per glide) half snowplow stop
  • Backward two-foot skating across width of ice (alternate push-glide sequence)

Novice 1

  • Forward spiral in a straight line - left foot
  • Forward spiral in a straight line - right foot
  • Backward one foot glide (four counts per glide, on alternate feet, minimum of four push-glide sequences)
  • Backward half snowplow stop (LBI)
  • Backward half snowplow stop (RBI)
  • Two-foot jump - backwards and forwards
  • LBI-RFI Mohawk
  • RBI-LFI Mohawk
  • Forward outside edge

Novice 2

  • Backward circle thrusts with RBO glide for two count- counter clockwise
  • Backward cross cuts- counter clockwise, minimum of six consecutive
  • Backward circle thrusts with LBO glide for two count- clockwise
  • Backward crosscuts- clockwise minimum of six consecutive
  • LBO-RFO Mohawk
  • RBO-LFO Mohawk
  • Waltz Jump
  • Forward inside edges
  • Slalom (edges or crosscuts, forwards and/or backwards)

Novice 3

  • LFO-LBI Three Jump
  • RFO-RBI Three Jump
  • Forward two-foot parallel side stop - left
  • Forward two-foot parallel side stop - right
  • Backward circle thrusts with LBO glide for four count - clockwise
  • Backward outside edges
  • Forward crosscuts/LFO-LBI three turn/backward crosscuts/RBO-LFO Mohawk on counter clockwise circle. Repeat minimum three times.
  • Forward crosscuts/RFO-RBI three turn/backward crosscuts/LBO-RFO Mohawk on clockwise circle. Repeat minimum of three times.
  • LFI-RBI Mohawk
  • Forward cross cuts/RFI-LBI Mohawk/backward crosscuts/LBI-RFI Mohawk on a counter clockwise circle. Repeat a minimum of three times.

Novice 4

  • Backward inside edges
  • LBO-LBI three turn/RBO edge on a counter clockwise circle. Repeat a minimum of four times
  • RFO-RBI three trun/LBO edge on a clockwise circle. Repeat a minimum of four times
  • LFI-LBO three turn
  • RFI-RBO three turn
  • LFI-LBO three jump
  • RFI-RBO three jump
  • Forward crosscuts in a figure 8 pattern
  • Backward crosscuts in a figure 8 pattern

Proficiency

  • Forward outside edges
  • Forward inside edges
  • Backwards outside edges
  • Backwards inside edges
  • Forward stroking two laps (crosscuts at the ends) RFO-RBI three turn or LFI-RBI Mohawk/backwards stroking two laps (crosscuts at the ends) - clockwise
  • Forward stroking two laps (crosscuts at the ends) LFO_LBI three turn or RFI-LBI Mohawk/backward stroking two laps (crosscuts at the ends) - counter clockwise

Power Skating Badges

Power 1

  • stopping- any method
  • forward two foot glide
  • forward scrootch down
  • forward double sculling
  • forward stroking with reasonable speed
  • jump over stick on ice- any method

Power 2

  • "v" push offs, both feet
  • Forward one foot glides- straight line, both feet
  • Forward running starts
  • Backwards skating- initial standard
  • Jump over stick on small pylons
  • Stopping- facing each direction

Power 3

  • backward stop
  • hockey stop- both directions, feet approaching parallel
  • backward double sculling
  • back one foot glides- both feet, straight line
  • forward crosscuts- both directions
  • jump over 2 consecutive hurdles
  • Forward inside and outside glides one foot glides on circle- both directions

Power 4

  • forward running starts
  • hockey stop -both directions -feet parallel
  • slide start
  • forward inside edges
  • forward and backward Mohawks on circle turning to the inside- both directions
  • backward Mohawk, turning to the outside

Power 5

  • Backward inside and outside one foot glides on a circle- both directions
  • Backward crosscuts- both directions
  • Forward outside edges
  • Forward crosscuts to backward crosscuts drill on circle- both directions
  • Hockey turns- both directions

Power 6

  • Backward inside edges - initial standard
  • Backward outside edges - initial standard
  • Forward sprint with crossover start
  • Backward sprint with crossover start
  • Fast forward and stopping on command
  • Forward slalom
  • Backward lateral motion

Power 7

  • Backward inside edges - advanced standard
  • Backward outside edges - advanced standard
  • Two foot jump over high hurdle
  • Leap over high hurdle
  • One foot mobility turns or three turns
  • Hockey turns
